About Me.

How it all began

Hi! My name is Malcom. I was born in Argentina and grew up in New York. Now, I live and work in the Dominican Republic. Ever since I was a child, I've loved technology and science. I've loved tinkering with electronics since I got my first computer. Even something as simple as editing batch files felt magical to a young me.

I remember how excited I was when I figured out I could edit Windows' autoexec.bat to echo a custom greeting for me that would show up every time I booted my machine. I also remember the feeling of utter dread after irreversibly altering or deleting an important system file, back during a time when file recovery and system restore points were not commonplace.

What I love

Technology and science were never my only passions.
